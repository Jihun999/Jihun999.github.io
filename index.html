Jihun Park

Hi, I'm Jihun Park, a M.S.-Ph.D. integrated course student at the Department of Artificial Intelligence at DGIST, South Korea. I am conducting my research under the supervision of Prof. Sunghoon Im at the DGIST Computer Vision Lab.

Previously, I earned a B.S. degree from the Department of Mechanical engineering, Zhejiang University, in 2022, and My research interest is computer vision and deep learning, with a particular emphasis on solving Autoregressive Image/Video Generation, Style Transfer, and Vision-Language Task.
